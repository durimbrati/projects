About Us

At Eris Generation Ltd, we specialize in groundworks, excavation, and foundation preparation for residential, commercial, and industrial developments across the UK. With over a decade of hands-on experience, our team delivers precision, safety, and efficiency on every site — no matter the complexity.

We combine modern equipment with proven methods to ensure stable, code-compliant foundations that stand the test of time. From site assessments and trenching to full substructure delivery, our work is built on transparency, technical excellence, and a deep respect for the environments we shape.
